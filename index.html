How Does Trežor Bridge Work?

Once installed, Trežor Bridge automatically detects when a Trežor device is connected and facilitates secure communication between your hardware wallet and Trežor Suite – the official desktop interface for Trežor users. It replaces the older Chrome extension method, offering a more stable and future-proof solution.
